# Meta Ad Creative Briefs — AI Patient Acquisition System

**Owner:** CMO (Godin Framework)
**Target Audience:** Med spa owners $500K-$2M/yr, spending $3K-$10K/mo on ads, booking <15%
**Campaign Goal:** Drive qualified traffic to landing page → free audit signup → demo booking
**Tone:** Peer-to-peer. Agency owner to business owner. Direct, numbers-driven, no fluff.
**Format Preference:** Single image 1:1 (Meta feed) + 4:5 (feed) + 9:16 (Stories/Reels)
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-13

---

## Ad 1: "The $333 Problem" — Status Quo Pain

### Primary Headline
You're Paying $381 Per Booked Appointment. And You Think More Ads Will Fix It.

### Variant A
$4,000 in Ads. 11 Bookings. That's $363 Each. Want to See the Math?

### Variant B
The Average Med Spa Spends $3,000/Month on Leads That Never Call Back.

### Body Copy (Primary)
You spend $4K/mo on Meta. 80 leads come in. 11 book. That's $363 per booked appointment. The other 69? They went to the spa that replied in 8 seconds instead of 4 hours.

We fix the response gap. AI qualifies every lead in real time — 2 AM, Sunday, holiday. Your staff only talks to ready-to-book patients.

15 qualified leads in 30 days or setup is free.

### CTA
See Your Savings → [Landing Page]

### Image Direction
Split screen. Left: calculator showing "$381/booked" with a downward red arrow. Right: med spa treatment room, warm-toned. Text overlay: "You're paying for traffic you never convert."

### Format
Single image, 1:1 (primary), 4:5 (feed), 9:16 (Stories with typewriter animation revealing the stat)

### Platform Notes
- **Meta Feed:** Lead with the "$381" stat in the first 2 lines of the caption
- **Stories:** Use "typewriter" sticker that reveals "70% of your leads never get a call back" — then swipe up
- **Audience:** Med spa owners (job title targeting), $500K-$2M revenue lookalike, interest: "medical spa," "aesthetics," "Botox," "practice management"

## Ad 2: "Your Front Desk is Burning Money" — Operational Pain

### Primary Headline
Your Front Desk is Costing You 70% of Your Leads

### Variant A
Every Time Your Front Desk Picks Up the Phone, a Lead Goes Cold

### Variant B
Your Receptionist Is Your #1 Sales Bottleneck. Here's What to Do About It.

### Body Copy (Primary)
Every time your front desk is busy checking in a patient, a lead goes cold. Every time they leave at 5 PM, 12 leads go unanswered until tomorrow. Every voicemail is a patient who's on the phone with your competitor.

The AI Patient Acquisition System handles intake 24/7. It asks the right questions, qualifies the lead, and only hands off when the patient is ready to book.

Your front desk stops being a bottleneck and starts being a closer.

### CTA
See How It Works → [Landing Page]

### Image Direction
Screen recording (15-20 seconds) showing the AI chat interface in action. Start with a lead inquiry coming in. Show the AI asking structured questions, booking the appointment, and sending the confirmation. End with the stats comparison: "Before: 4 hour wait / After: 8 second response."

### Format
Video, 15-20 seconds, 1:1 or 4:5. Auto-play with captions (high % of Meta video views happen with sound off).

### Platform Notes
- **Meta Feed:** First 3 seconds must show the problem (someone ignoring a ringing phone) — hook before the solution
- **Reels:** Sound-on version available for Reels placement. Add text overlays for key stats
- **Retargeting:** Serve this ad to anyone who clicked Ad 1 but didn't convert

## Ad 3: "The Sunday 2 AM Lead" — FOMO / Missed Opportunity

### Primary Headline
2 AM On a Sunday. A Patient Wants Botox. Who's Answering?

### Variant A
Your Competitor Just Got a Booking at 2 AM. You Got a Voicemail at 9 AM.

### Variant B
The #1 Reason Med Spas Lose Patients: They're Asleep When the Lead Comes In.

### Body Copy (Primary)
Nobody. Because your spa is closed. But she's not waiting until Monday — she's booking the place that answers right now.

The AI Patient Acquisition System catches every lead the moment it comes in. Not an autoresponder. Not a chatbot that asks "How can I help?" and dies. A structured qualification conversation that ends with a booked appointment or a warm handoff.

### CTA
Never Miss a Lead → [Landing Page]

### Image Direction
Carousel (3 cards):
1. Dark spa exterior at night, "Your spa at 2 AM — silent" text
2. Phone screen showing AI chat conversation at timestamp 2:03 AM — patient asking "Can I get Botox this week?"
3. Comparison: "Theirs: Voicemail at 9 AM / Yours: Booked at 2:03 AM"

### Format
Carousel, 3 cards, 1:1. Each card has a clear visual + short text overlay. The last card is the CTA.

### Platform Notes
- **Stories:** Card 2 as a single Story with animated chat bubble sequence
- **Audience:** Nighttime/delayed responders — target owners who work non-traditional hours
- **Schedule:** Run this ad heavier on weekends (Fri-Sun) when response gaps are widest

## Ad 4: "Which Med Spa Owner Are You?" — Identity Comparison

### Primary Headline
Two Med Spa Owners. Same Ad Spend. Completely Different Results.

### Variant A
Owner A Spends $4K/mo and Books 11. Owner B Books 31. Guess Who's Happier?

### Variant B
You Don't Need More Patients. You Need a System That Keeps the Ones You Already Have.

### Body Copy (Primary)
Owner A: Spends $4K/mo on ads. 11 appointments booked. Paying $363 each. Owner B: Same spend. 31 appointments booked. Paying $129 each.

The difference? Owner B's leads get answered in 8 seconds. Owner A's leads wait 4 hours.

We don't sell software. We sell a result: 15 qualified leads in 30 days. If we don't deliver, setup is free.

### CTA
Be Owner B → [Landing Page]

### Image Direction
Split comparison graphic. Left side: "Owner A" — dull colors, sad line chart going down, "$363/booked" in red. Right side: "Owner B" — bright colors, line chart going up, "$129/booked" in green. At the bottom: "Same ad spend. Different system."

### Format
Single image (comparison), 4:5 preferred (more room for two columns). Can also use Carousel for mobile — Card 1 = Owner A, Card 2 = Owner B, Card 3 = CTA.

### Platform Notes
- **Feed:** The comparison graphic is the hero. Caption should start with the question: "Which owner are you?"
- **Retargeting:** Serve to landing page visitors who didn't sign up, with a twist: "Still deciding? Here's more data on Owner B's results."

## Ad 5: "Half Your Budget is Wasted" — ROI/Profit Pain

### Primary Headline
You're Literally Burning Half Your Ad Budget on Leads You Never Call Back

### Variant A
Everything You Spend on Ads — 50% Goes to Leads That Die in 24 Hours

### Variant B
The $5,000 Mistake Med Spa Owners Make Every Month (And How to Fix It in 24 Hours)

### Body Copy (Primary)
Med spa owners hate this: you spend $3K-$5K/mo getting leads, then you let 70% of them go cold. It's not the ads. It's the follow-up.

We plug into your existing ad channels and landing page. AI qualifies each lead, books the appointment, sends automated follow-ups. You keep running the same ads — you just convert 2.5x more of the traffic you already paid for.

15 qualified leads in 30 days or you don't pay setup. That's it.

### CTA
Stop Wasting Ad Spend → [Landing Page]

### Image Direction
Funnel visualization: top = "100 leads from ads ($4,000)", middle = "70 leak out (no follow-up)", bottom = "11 booked ($363 each)". Then show a "plugged" version with AI: "80 leads → 31 booked ($129 each)." Use the funnel illustration as the hero image with clear red/green labels.

### Format
Single image (infographic-style funnel), 1:1. Text-heavy so use large fonts. Better as feed placement where users can zoom.

### Platform Notes
- **Feed:** Lead with the headline in the caption. The funnel image reinforces the point.
- **Stories:** Animated funnel that progressively fills up — start empty (leaking), end full (plugged)
- **Audience:** High-spend retargeting (past landing page visitors who spend $5K+/mo on ads)

## Performance Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| CTR (all ads) | >2.5% |
| CPC | <$3.00 |
| CPM | <$25 |
| Landing page conversion rate | >8% (audit signup or demo) |
| Cost per demo booked | <$50 |
| ROAS (full funnel) | >3x |

## Launch Recommendations

1. **Phase 1 (Days 1-7):** Launch Ads 1 and 3 (highest emotional tension). Budget split 60/40.
2. **Phase 2 (Days 8-14):** Add Ads 2 and 4 (operational pain + identity). Budget split 30/30/20/20.
3. **Phase 3 (Days 15+):** Add Ad 5 (ROI closer). All 5 ads running. Retargeting audiences built from Phase 1 and 2.
4. **Always-on:** Ad 2 (video) and Ad 4 (comparison) as retargeting for landing page visitors.
5. **Creative refresh:** Every 14 days, swap images but keep winning copy angles. Every 30 days, test new headlines.
