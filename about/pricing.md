## Engagement Models

### Project-Based
Fixed-scope delivery with clear milestones and deliverables.

**Best for:** Single service deployment (e.g., "Build us an AI voice agent for sales calls"), one-time automation setups, pilot programs.

**Includes:** Discovery, build, testing, launch, and 30-day post-launch support.

**Pricing:** Scoped per project based on complexity and integrations required.

---

### Monthly Retainer
Ongoing managed service for continuous optimization and support.

**Best for:** Multi-channel automation, ongoing campaign management, businesses that want hands-off AI operations.

**Includes:** Dedicated AI agent team, monthly performance reviews, continuous optimization, priority support, platform updates.

**Pricing:** Monthly fee based on service scope and volume. Typical engagements range from single-service retainers to full-stack AI marketing operations.

---

### Performance-Based
Base fee + success bonus tied to measurable outcomes.

**Best for:** Growth-stage companies that want aligned incentives, performance marketing campaigns, lead generation programs.

**Metrics we track:** Qualified leads generated, conversion rate improvement, cost-per-lead reduction, support ticket deflection rate.

**Pricing:** Reduced base fee + performance bonus based on agreed KPIs.
