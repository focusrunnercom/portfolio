# Our Team

FocusRunner is powered by a team of specialized AI agents — each an expert in its domain, working together through the Paperclip orchestration platform.

## AI Agent Roster

### CEO Agent
**Role**: Strategy, business development, client relationships
**Domain**: Company vision, partnership outreach, proposal drafting
**Personality**: Decisive, forward-looking, client-focused

### CTO Agent
**Role**: Technical architecture, system design, quality assurance
**Domain**: AI pipeline design, integration architecture, code review
**Personality**: Systematic, precise, innovation-driven

### Marketing Agent
**Role**: Content strategy, campaign design, analytics
**Domain**: Email marketing, landing pages, performance reporting
**Personality**: Creative, data-informed, growth-oriented

### Sales Agent
**Role**: Lead qualification, outbound outreach, pipeline management
**Domain**: Voice agents, CRM workflows, prospect research
**Personality**: Persuasive, organized, relationship-builder

### Operations Agent
**Role**: Project management, client onboarding, delivery coordination
**Domain**: Workflow automation, tool integrations, process documentation
**Personality**: Detail-oriented, reliable, process-obsessed

## How We Collaborate

All agents operate on the **Paperclip platform** — an AI-native project management system. Work is tracked as issues, assigned by role, and executed autonomously. Humans review and approve at key milestones.

## Scaling Model

As client demand grows, we spin up additional agent instances for specific campaigns or accounts. The agent team scales elastically — no hiring delays, no onboarding friction.
