## DIAGNOSIS: The Real Obstacle

Med spas spend $3K–$10K/month on ads but book fewer than 15% of leads. The bottleneck is not traffic — it's lead conversion speed and follow-up. 70% of med spa leads go cold within 24 hours because:

1. **Response time is hours, not seconds.** Front desk staff reply during business hours. Leads expect instant responses.
2. **No systematic qualification.** Every lead gets the same inconsistent manual screening. Best prospects slip through when staff is busy.
3. **No follow-up beyond 1 text.** Unconverted leads get nothing. They book Botox at the spa that stayed in touch.

The med spa owner's pain: "I spend $4K on ads, get 80 leads, book 11. $363 per booked consult. I can't scale because my cost per booked appointment stays flat — I just burn more money on ads."

**The leverage point:** Response time. Move from 4-hour average to 8-second average and double the booking rate without increasing ad spend.

---

## GUIDING POLICY: One Approach That Wins

**Productize patient acquisition as a flat-fee AI service with a guarantee.**

We do not sell software. We sell a result: 15 qualified, ready-to-book leads in 30 days. If we don't deliver, setup is free.

This policy wins because:
- **Flat fee** ($2.5K setup + $2.5K/mo) is easy to decide on. No demo-to-contract dance.
- **Guarantee absorbs risk.** The med spa owner doesn't gamble.
- **Volume is our moat.** Every client trains the AI better. Our system gets smarter, our cost per lead drops, our margin expands.
- **We own the patient relationship.** If the AI qualifies the lead, the med spa books them. They don't churn the system.

## What We Say No To

1. **No custom builds.** Every client gets the same system. Configuration is 7 days, not 8 weeks.
2. **No SaaS model.** We tried it. Agency wins. $2.5K/mo recurring with $50K+ annual value per client.
3. **No enterprise.** Med spas making $500K–$2M. Not hospitals. Not chains (unless franchise-by-franchise).
4. **No organic-first strategy.** Ads are the engine. We optimize ad spend — not build audiences from scratch.
5. **No other verticals until med spa is proven.** 3 paying med spa clients at $2.5K/mo = $7.5K MRR. Proof of repeatability. Then expand.
