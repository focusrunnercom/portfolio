## Abandonment Rule

- No response after Touch 5 → Move to "cold nurture" (monthly newsletter + quarterly case study email)
- Reply at any touch → Human takes over immediately (Sales agent triggers)
- Book demo at any touch → Remove from sequence, enter CRM as "warm lead"

---

## Compliance Notes

- **CAN-SPAM:** Include physical address (FocusRunner HQ) in all emails. Unsubscribe link in footer.
- **TCPA:** SMS Touch 2 must include opt-out instructions ("Reply STOP to opt out"). GHL handles this automatically.
- **Timing:** Emails send at 10 AM local time (highest open rate for B2B). SMS at 12 PM (lunch break) or 6 PM (post-work).
- **Frequency cap:** Max 2 touches in any 48-hour window.
