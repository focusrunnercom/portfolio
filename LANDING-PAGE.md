## 7. Copy Directives for Copywriter

**Tone:** Peer-to-peer (agency owner to med spa owner). Not vendor-to-client.
**Voice:** Direct, confident, slightly aggressive. Like a business consultant who's seen this exact problem 50 times.
**Pacing:**
- Hero: Punchy. 1-2 sentences max.
- Problem: Slightly longer. Paint the pain vividly.
- Solution: Bullet-point clean. Show don't tell.
- Guarantee: Bold. Short sentences. Periods.
- Pricing: Minimal. Let the numbers do the work.
**Length:** ~1,200 words total (mobile-friendly). Under 800 words above the fold.

**Key phrases to use:**
- "Same ads. Same traffic. Different system."
- "Not a chatbot. A qualification system."
- "15 leads in 30 days or setup is free."
- "You don't need more traffic. You need a system that captures what you already paid for."

**Key phrases to avoid:**
- "Revolutionize your practice" (overbaked)
- "Cutting-edge AI" (everyone says this)
- "Game-changer" (meaningless)
- "Book a call" (too weak — "Book a demo" is specific)
