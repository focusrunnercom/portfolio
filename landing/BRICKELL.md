## Three pain points holding you back

### 1. 15+ spas in 2 miles
Brickell is saturated. Your pricing is nearly identical to everyone else's. Your services overlap. The only real differentiator left is speed — who answers the phone, who replies to the DM, who books the consult first.

### 2. Pricing is identical, experience isn't
You can't win a price war in Brickell — every practice runs the same seasonal promos. But you *can* win the responsiveness war. The clinic that converts a lead in under 60 seconds closes at 3x the rate of the one that replies in 5 minutes.

### 3. Who responds first wins
Every hour of delay is a patient lost to a competitor. Brickell prospects shop around. They send 3 DMs, fill out 2 contact forms, and book whoever replies first. If your follow-up takes longer than a lunch break, you've already lost.
