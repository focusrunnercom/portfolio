## 2. Landing Page Structure

Reference full copy in LANDING-PAGE.md. This section defines the structure and Godin reasoning.

| Section | Godin Principle | Key Line |
|---------|----------------|----------|
| Hero | Tension → Resolution | "Your Med Spa Is Losing $2,550/Month to Cold Leads" |
| Stats Bar | Social Proof (People Like Us) | 92% response rate vs 28% industry avg |
| Problem | Emotional Labor | 4 pain points: cold leads, ad bleed, manual qual, no follow-up |
| Solution | Permission Marketing | AI qualifies 24/7 — list of 5 capabilities |
| How It Works | Ship Creative Work | 3 steps: Audit → Install → Grow |
| Guarantee | Tension Released | "15 Leads in 30 Days or It's Free" |
| Case Study | People Like Us Do Things Like This | Scottsdale med spa: $381 → $135 per booked |
| ICP Fit | Funnel is a Filter | "It's for you if..." vs "Not for you if..." table |
| Pricing | Smallest Viable Market | $2.5K + $2.5K/mo, no contracts |
| Demo CTA | Permission Marketing | 15-min demo, 3 outcomes listed |

**Layout Optimization:**
- Mobile-first. Med spa owners browse on phone between procedures.
- One CTA per fold. "Book Demo" is the only exit.
- Trust signals near CTA: guarantee badge + stat bar.
- Form: 4 fields (name, email, phone, treatment interest). Longer forms kill conversion at this price point.

## 4. Follow-Up Sequence: 5-Touch SMS/Email

**Trigger:** Lead completes form but doesn't book demo within 24 hours
**Platform:** GoHighLevel campaign (configured by CTO/Engineer)
**Tone:** Permission Marketing — earned attention, not spam

### Touch 1: Day 1 — Value (Email)
**Subject:** Your Free Patient Acquisition Audit
**Body:**
- Link to download the audit PDF
- 3 quick wins they can implement today (no AI needed)
- "Reply with your current cost per booked appointment and I'll tell you if our system would work for you"

### Touch 2: Day 2 — Social Proof (SMS)
**Text:**
"Hey [Name], quick question — what's your current cost per booked appointment? For most med spas it's $300-400. Our clients drop to ~$135. If you're above $200, worth a 15-min chat. — Alex, FocusRunner"

### Touch 3: Day 4 — Case Study (Email)
**Subject:** How a Scottsdale med spa went from 11 to 31 booked appointments
**Body:**
- The full case study (already in LANDING-PAGE.md)
- Key metrics: $4.2K same ad spend, 2.8x more booked appointments
- "Same ad spend. Same city. Different system."

### Touch 4: Day 7 — Objection Handling (SMS)
**Text:**
"Most med spa owners worry the AI will sound robotic or scare patients. Fair concern. Here's a real transcript from our system. Read it and tell me if this sounds like a real person or a chatbot. [Link to transcript] — Alex, FocusRunner"

### Touch 5: Day 14 — Final Offer (SMS + Email)
**Subject:** Last chance for priority setup (3-5 days instead of 7)
**Body:**
- First 5 clients get priority onboarding
- Guarantee: 15 leads in 30 days or setup refunded
- "Book a 15-min demo this week and we'll have your system live by [Day+7]"
- Calendar link + direct reply option

### Abandonment Rule
- No response after Touch 5 → Move to "cold nurture" (monthly case study emails)
- Reply at any touch → Human takes over immediately (Sales agent triggers)
