## Diagnosis: The Three Gaps

### 1. Response Time Gap
- **Current:** ___ hours
- **Target:** <60 seconds
- **Impact:** 78% of patients book the first spa that responds. Every hour you wait, a patient books elsewhere.
- **Cost:** For every 100 leads, 70 go to the fastest responder. If that's not you, that's 70 lost patients/month.

### 2. Follow-Up Gap
- **Current:** ___ touches per lead
- **Target:** 5+ touches over 30 days
- **Impact:** 80% of sales require 5 follow-ups. Most med spas stop at 1. The difference between an 11% booking rate and 38% booking rate is a systematic follow-up.
- **Cost:** Every lead that didn't book on first contact but would have after 5 touches is revenue walking out the door.

### 3. Qualification Gap
- **Current:** Manual, inconsistent
- **Target:** Structured AI qualification
- **Impact:** Front desk spends 60%+ of time answering "how much is X?" from people who never book. High-value patients (Botox, laser, filler at $500-$2K per visit) get the same response as low-value inquiries.
- **Cost:** Your highest-value patients get lost in the same noise as tire-kickers.
