# Email Marketing

**Hyper-personalized campaigns driven by AI — not templates.**

---

## Overview

We build AI-powered email marketing systems that generate personalized content for every recipient. No more generic newsletters — every email is tailored to the individual's behavior, preferences, and stage in the buyer journey.

## What We Offer

### AI-Generated Content
- Subject lines optimized for open rates by reinforcement learning
- Body copy tailored to recipient's industry, role, and past interactions
- Dynamic product recommendations based on browsing and purchase history

### Behavioral Automation
- Trigger-based flows: abandoned cart, welcome series, re-engagement
- Predictive send-time optimization per recipient
- Automatic list segmentation based on engagement signals

### Performance Optimization
- Continuous A/B testing of subject lines, CTAs, and layouts
- Deliverability monitoring and reputation management
- Spam compliance (CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL)

## Stack

| Layer | Technology |
|---|---|
| Content Generation | Custom GPT pipelines with brand voice training |
| Delivery | SendGrid, Amazon SES, Mailgun |
| Automation | n8n workflows with custom AI nodes |
| Analytics | Custom dashboards with cohort analysis |
| Personalization | Behavioral tracking + CRM data fusion |

## Deliverables

Every engagement includes:

- **Strategy document** — Audience segmentation, content pillars, KPIs
- **Template library** — 5–10 responsive email templates (branded)
- **Automation flows** — 3–5 trigger-based sequences deployed and tested
- **Dashboard** — Real-time open rates, click rates, conversions, revenue attribution
- **Monthly report** — Performance analysis with AI-driven recommendations

## Results We've Driven

- 35–55% average open rate improvement vs. industry benchmarks
- 20–40% increase in click-through rates
- 15–25% revenue lift from email channel within 90 days
