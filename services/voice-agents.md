# AI Voice Agents

**Intelligent voice bots that handle calls so your team doesn't have to.**

---

## Overview

Our AI voice agents are designed to conduct natural, human-like phone conversations. They handle inbound and outbound calls for sales, lead qualification, and customer support — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

## What They Do

### Outbound Sales Calls
- Cold and warm outreach at scale
- Consistent pitch delivery with adaptive objection handling
- Real-time lead scoring during calls

### Lead Qualification
- Automated discovery calls that qualify leads against your ICP
- Instant CRM updates with call summaries and next steps
- Multi-language support for international campaigns

### Customer Support
- 24/7 first-line support for common inquiries
- Intelligent escalation to human agents when needed
- Post-call sentiment analysis and ticket creation

## Technology Stack

| Component | Technology |
|---|---|
| Speech-to-Text | Whisper (real-time streaming) |
| Conversation Engine | Custom LLM pipelines with RAG |
| Text-to-Speech | Neural TTS with natural prosody |
| Telephony | SIP trunking / Twilio integration |
| Analytics | Call scoring, sentiment tracking, conversion dashboards |

## Integrations

- **CRM**: HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive
- **Helpdesk**: Zendesk, Intercom
- **Telephony**: Twilio, Vonage, custom SIP
- **Analytics**: Custom dashboards with real-time metrics

## Pricing

Custom pricing based on call volume, complexity, and integrations required. Typical deployments range from 500 to 50,000 calls per month.
