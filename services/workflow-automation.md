# Workflow Automation

**End-to-end marketing pipelines that run on autopilot.**

---

## Overview

We build autonomous marketing workflows that connect your tools, process your data, and trigger actions — without human intervention. From lead capture to closed deal, every step is automated.

## Capabilities

### Lead Pipeline Automation
- Multi-channel lead capture (web forms, chatbots, social media)
- Automatic enrichment from 30+ data sources
- Intelligent routing to the right sales rep or AI agent

### Campaign Orchestration
- Multi-channel drip sequences (email, SMS, LinkedIn)
- Behavioral triggers based on site visits, email opens, and form submissions
- A/B testing with automatic winner selection

### Data Synchronization
- Real-time bidirectional sync between CRM, email platform, and analytics
- Custom field mapping and transformation rules
- Conflict resolution and deduplication logic

### Reporting & Alerts
- Automated weekly performance digests
- Anomaly detection with Slack/email alerts
- Custom KPI dashboards updated in real time

## How We Build It

1. **Audit** — We map your current marketing stack and identify gaps.
2. **Design** — Our AI agents design the workflow architecture.
3. **Build** — We implement using n8n, Make, or custom code — whichever fits best.
4. **Test** — Full end-to-end testing with synthetic data.
5. **Monitor** — Ongoing performance tracking and optimization.
