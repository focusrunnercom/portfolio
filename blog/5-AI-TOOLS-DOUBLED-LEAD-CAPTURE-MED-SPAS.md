## 2. ChatGPT / Claude — Content & Script Generation

**Best for:** Creating ad copy, email sequences, and response scripts.
**Pricing:** $20/month (ChatGPT Plus) or API-based.

General-purpose AI is surprisingly good at drafting med spa marketing copy — email sequences for abandoned bookings, Instagram caption variations, and response templates for common lead questions.

**Key use cases:**
- Draft 10 variations of a Google Ads headline in 30 seconds
- Write a 5-email nurture sequence for cold leads
- Generate FAQ responses for common pricing and treatment questions
- Create personalized follow-up messages based on treatment interest

**Limitation:** No automation. You still need to paste the output into your tools.

## 3. Calendly — Automated Scheduling

**Best for:** Removing the "Can we find a time?" back-and-forth.
**Pricing:** Free tier available; paid plans start at $10/month.

Calendly integrates with your Google or Outlook calendar and lets leads book directly into available slots. No phone tag. No "let me check with our scheduler."

**Pro tip:** Put a Calendly link in your automated response. Leads who see "Book a consult here" within 30 seconds of submitting a form convert at much higher rates than leads who wait for a phone call.

## 4. Zapier — Pipeline Automation

**Best for:** Connecting your lead capture tools together.
**Pricing:** Free tier (100 tasks/month); paid plans start at $25/month.

Zapier is the glue. Connect your form submissions (Google Forms, Typeform, GHL) → CRM → Slack notification → SMS alert.

**Key med spa automation:**
- New form submission → Add to Google Sheets + Send Slack notification to front desk
- Lead scores Hot → Create Calendly invite link + Send SMS to lead
- Lead fills out "interested in X treatment" → Add to email sequence for X treatment

## 5. Google Business Profile — Zero-Cost Lead Generation

**Best for:** Local SEO and getting found by "near me" searches.
**Pricing:** Free.

This is the most underutilized lead generation tool in med spa marketing. Google Business Profile (GBP) is the first thing potential patients see when they search "med spa Miami" or "Botox near me."

**Optimization checklist:**
- Complete every field (services, hours, photos, Q&A)
- Respond to every review within 24 hours
- Post weekly updates (new treatments, specials, before/after)
- Use GBP Q&A to answer common questions proactively
