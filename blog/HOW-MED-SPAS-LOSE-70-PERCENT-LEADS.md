# How Med Spas Lose 70% of Leads (And What to Do About It)

You're spending thousands on Google Ads, Instagram, and SEO. Leads come in: form submissions, DMs, phone calls. Then they disappear.

Here's a number that should stop you cold: **70% of med spa leads never book a consultation.** They fill out a form, reach out on Instagram, or call your front desk — and then nothing happens.

The lead isn't bad. The follow-up is.

## The $15,000 Leak

Let's run the math on a typical Miami med spa:

- Monthly ad spend: $3,000 - $5,000
- Monthly leads generated: 50 - 80
- Leads that actually book: 15 - 24
- Lost leads per month: 35 - 56
- Average consult value: $300
- **Monthly revenue lost: $10,500 - $16,800**

That's not a marketing problem. That's a response-time problem — and it's bleeding six figures a year out of your practice.

## Why Med Spas Are Especially Vulnerable

Med spas sit at a unique intersection that makes lead loss worse than in other industries:

**1. High-intent traffic.** Someone searching for "laser hair removal Miami" or "Botox near me" is ready to book. They're not browsing. They're buying. If you don't answer in minutes, they call the next clinic on the list.

**2. Price-sensitive buyers.** Spa treatments are elective spend. When a lead hesitates for 15 minutes waiting for a reply, the emotional purchase window closes. "I'll think about it" means "I won't come back."

**3. Understaffed front desks.** Most med spas run lean. The front desk person is booking appointments, answering phones, checking patients in, and processing payments. They cannot respond to every web lead within 60 seconds. By the time they get to the lead queue, it's too late.

## The Data: Response Time vs. Close Rate

Industry benchmarks tell a brutal story:

| Response Time | Close Rate |
|---------------|-----------|
| Under 1 minute | 80%+ |
| 1 - 5 minutes | 60%+ |
| 5 - 30 minutes | ~30% |
| 30 - 60 minutes | ~15% |
| 1 - 4 hours | < 5% |
| Over 4 hours | < 1% |

The average med spa responds to web leads in **4-6 hours**.

That means if you're running ads, you're paying to generate leads that walk out the door before your front desk even sees them. You're burning $3-5K/month on ad spend and capturing less than 30% of the value.

## The Fix Is Not More Staff

You can't hire someone to watch the form submissions 24/7. The margins don't support it, and the work is mind-numbing — answering the same pricing questions 50 times a day.

The fix is a **triage layer** between the form submission and the front desk:

1. **Instant reply.** Within 3 seconds of a lead coming in, an automated system acknowledges them, answers common questions (pricing, hours, services), and gathers qualification info.

2. **Smart routing.** Hot leads (ready to book this week) go straight to your scheduler. Warm leads get a nurture sequence. Cold leads get drip content.

3. **24/7 operation.** Leads that come in at 2 AM get treated the same as leads at 2 PM. No lost overnight traffic.

4. **Lead scoring built in.** Every lead gets a score based on treatment interest, timeline, budget, and location. Your front desk sees a prioritized queue, not a spreadsheet of 50 names.

## What Recovery Looks Like

A Miami med spa we worked with had exactly this problem: $4K/month in ads, 60 leads/month, 12 consultations booked. **80% of leads were evaporating.**

After implementing instant lead qualification:

- **Leads scored and triaged automatically**
- **Response time dropped from 4 hours to under 10 seconds**
- **Bookings went from 12 to 47 per month** in 90 days
- **Cost per booked lead dropped from $333 to $85**

Same ad spend. Same services. Same staff. The only change was how fast they answered.
