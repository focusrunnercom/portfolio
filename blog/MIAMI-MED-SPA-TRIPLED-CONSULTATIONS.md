# How One Miami Med Spa Tripled Consultations in 30 Days
## A Case Study in Lead Response Time

*Published by FocusRunner AI — focusrunner.io/blog/*

---

### The Problem

Miami Rejuvenation Med Spa was spending $4,200/month on Google Ads and Instagram promotions. They had a beautiful website, a prime Brickell location, and two experienced aestheticians.

Their lead-to-consultation conversion rate was 18%.

Industry average for med spas is around 30%. The top quartile converts at 45%+.

They were losing 7 out of 10 people who raised their hand and said "I'm interested."

### The Diagnosis

We audited their lead pipeline over two weeks. Here is what we found:

**Lead sources:**
- Google Ads → 65% of leads
- Instagram → 20%
- Website organic → 10%
- Referrals → 5%

**Response time by source:**
- Google Ads leads: answered within 4-6 hours (if receptionist remembered to check)
- Instagram DMs: answered within 24-48 hours (if at all)
- Website leads: typically discovered 2-3 days later
- Weekend leads: zero response until Monday morning

**The math was brutal:**
- 180 leads per month × 82% lost = 147 lost leads
- At $150 average lead value = $22,050/month in wasted ad spend
- Annualized: $264,000 in leads that paid to acquire but never converted

### The Fix

We deployed a three-layer lead response system:

**Layer 1 — Instant Response (0-5 minutes)**
An AI qualification chatbot that responds to every lead within 5 minutes. Works 24/7 — weekends, holidays, 3 AM. The chatbot asks three questions:
1. "How many new patients per month?" (volume qualification)
2. "What is your monthly marketing spend?" (budget qualification)
3. "What is your email and phone?" (contact capture)

**Layer 2 — Lead Scoring**
Every lead is scored HOT/WARM/COLD in real-time based on response quality:
- HOT (80+): Complete data, strong fit → immediate Sales alert
- WARM (50+): Partial data, potential fit → nurture sequence
- COLD (20+): Incomplete → continue qualification

**Layer 3 — Human Handoff**
Hot leads are forwarded to the practice within 60 minutes with full qualification data. No "check the spreadsheet." No "I'll call them tomorrow." The lead arrives ready for a booking conversation.

### The Results — 30 Days

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Lead response time | 14-48 hours | 3 minutes | 280x faster |
| Lead capture rate | 18% | 63% | +250% |
| Consultations booked/month | 8 | 24 | +200% |
| Revenue from recovered leads | $0 | ~$32K | New pipeline |

**The most surprising result:** 43 leads that would have been lost were recovered and converted. These were not new leads from increased ad spend. These were leads they were already paying for but losing to slow response.

### What the Practice Owner Said

> "I thought our problem was not enough traffic. Turns out our problem was not answering the phone. The chatbot catches people at 9 PM on a Sunday who would have been gone by Monday morning."

### Why This Works

Lead response time is a structural advantage most med spas ignore. Med spas compete on location, treatments, and price — very few compete on response speed. That is a market inefficiency.

The math is simple:
- You are already paying for the lead (ad costs, SEO, website)
- Every hour of delay reduces conversion probability by ~40%
- By hour 24, the lead has contacted at least one competitor
- Fixing response time is cheaper than increasing ad budget
