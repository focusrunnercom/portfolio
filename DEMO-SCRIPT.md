# FocusRunner Demo Script — AI Patient Acquisition System Walkthrough

> Target: Med Spa Owners ($500K–$2M revenue)
> Offer: AI Patient Acquisition System — $2.5K setup + $1.5K-$2.5K/mo
> Duration: 15-20 minutes (not 30 — respect their time)
> Framework: Challenger (Teach → Tailor → Take Control)
> Pre-requisite: They've said yes to "see how it works" (cold DM or call converted to demo)

---

## Preparation (5 min — mandatory before every demo)

Know these things before starting:
1. Their current ad spend (from discovery call)
2. Their stated booking rate / lead volume
3. Number of providers and locations
4. Their stated biggest frustration
5. Their competitor landscape (who else in their city uses AI?)

If you don't have items 1-4, the demo is a general pitch, not a tailored sale. Don't do it.

---

## Phase 1: Set the Frame (0:00 – 2:00)

Purpose: Establish that this is a business improvement, not a tech demo. They don't care about AI. They care about booked consults.

**Script:**

"Thanks for taking the time, [Name]. I know you're busy.

Here's what I want to accomplish in the next 15 minutes:
1. Show you exactly where your revenue is leaking right now
2. Walk you through how we fix it — specifically for YOUR practice
3. You decide if it makes sense

If at any point you think this doesn't apply, tell me and we're done. Fair?"

**[Wait for "fair" or "yes"]**

"Great. Just to confirm what you told me last time — you're spending about $[X] on ads and booking about [Y] consults per month, right?"

**[Confirm numbers or get updated ones]**

---

## Phase 2: The Problem — Revenue Leak Visualization (2:00 – 5:00)

Purpose: Visually show them the gap. Use THEIR numbers. Make it undeniable.

**Script:**

"Let me show you what's happening with your leads right now.

I pulled some data on this. Industry benchmark: med spas that respond to leads within 60 seconds book 78% of consults. Those that take 4+ hours (which is the industry average) book 22%.

When you said your leads come in through Instagram DMs and website forms, and your front desk gets to them when they have time — that's the 4+ hour bucket.

So here's what that looks like for YOUR numbers:
- You get [X] leads per month at [$Y] ad spend
- ~[80%] go cold within 24 hours because no one responded fast enough
- At $[avg consult value], that's $[Z] in potential revenue walking out the door every month

**[Show calculation on screen or in a simple spreadsheet]**

The question isn't whether your ads are working. It's whether your response system is catching the people your ads paid to attract. Right now, it's not."

---

## Phase 3: The Solution — Show, Don't Sell (5:00 – 10:00)

Purpose: Walk through the system quickly. Less is more. Don't show every feature. Show the 3 that solve their specific pain.

**Three-tier demonstration (choose based on their stated frustration):**

### Tier A — They said "leads go cold"
Show: AI auto-response in action
- "A lead comes in at 9 PM asking about CoolSculpting pricing"
- "Our AI responds in under 90 seconds with pricing, availability, and a booking link"
- "The lead books a consult. Your front desk sees it on the calendar tomorrow morning — already qualified"

### Tier B — They said "staff is overwhelmed"
Show: Qualification + routing
- "AI asks 3 questions: budget range, treatment interest, timeframe"
- "Only leads with budget $500+ and 'ready this month' reach a human"
- "Your staff only talks to people who will actually book"

### Tier C — They said "I don't know where leads come from"
Show: Attribution dashboard
- "Every booked consult is tagged with source channel, time of day, and response time"
- "You see exactly what's working. No more guessing."

**Transition:**

"That's it. Three things that run 24/7. No extra staff. No training. We deploy in 5 days."

---

## Phase 4: Objection Handling (10:00 – 13:00)

Anticipate. Don't wait for them to bring it up.

**Script — pre-empt the top 3:**

"At this point, most owners I talk to have three concerns. Let me address them directly:

**1. 'Will this actually work for MY practice?'**
Every system is pre-configured for your treatments, pricing, and booking flow. It learns your FAQ patterns within the first week. If a lead asks about Botox vs Dysport, the AI knows the difference because we loaded your price list and service menu.

**2. 'Is this another thing I have to manage?'**
Zero. Your staff doesn't touch it. If something breaks, we fix it same day. You get one weekly report. That's it.

**3. 'Can I cancel if it doesn't work?'**
Yes. No contract. Cancel any time. If you're not seeing more booked consults in 30 days, you walk away. We take the risk."

---

## Phase 5: The Close (13:00 – 15:00)

Purpose: Direct ask. No pressure, but no ambiguity.

**Script:**

"So here's what I'd suggest:

We deploy the full system in 5 days. $2,500 setup, $2,500/month after that. Includes the AI lead capture, qualification, booking, and review management.

If in 30 days you're not seeing 30-50% more booked consults, you cancel. No fee. No hard feelings.

I have a deployment slot open for next [day of week]. Can I get you set up?"

**If yes:** "Great. I'll send you the intake form. Takes 10 minutes. We'll start the setup."
**If not yet:** "What's the one thing that needs to be true for you to say yes?" (Find the real objection, then handle it.)

---

## Post-Demo Actions

After every demo, immediately:
1. Send a follow-up email within 2 hours summarizing exactly what was covered
2. Include the revenue leak calculation with their numbers
3. Propose 3 specific dates for deployment
4. Add to CRM with stage: Demo Completed
5. If no decision: schedule follow-up call for 48 hours later
