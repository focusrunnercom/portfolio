# FocusRunner Follow-Up Sequence — Med Spa Outreach

> Target: Med Spa owners who said "not now" or "not interested" after initial contact
> Duration: 90-day sequence (6 touches maximum — don't over-pursue)
> Tone: Value-first. No "just checking in." Every message must teach something.
> Channel: Email preferred. IG DM for warm leads only.

---

## Follow-Up Rules

1. **Never send "just checking in."** Every message teaches or adds value.
2. **Personalize every email.** Reference THEIR numbers, THEIR city, THEIR objection.
3. **Track opens.** If they open 3+ emails, re-engage with a direct offer.
4. **Stop after Touch 6.** Over-pursuit damages your brand. Leave the door open.
5. **Respond instantly to any reply.** If they reply to any of these, drop the sequence and engage directly.
