// The Problem

Smile Architects was running Google Ads for "veneers scottsdale" — good keyword, $1,200/month budget. But their landing page was a generic "Call Us" form. Visitors would click the ad, see a boring page, and bounce. 2.3% conversion rate. $87 cost per consultation. At $87/consultation with a 20% close rate on veneers ($8K avg case), the math barely worked. Dr. Chen knew he was burning money but didn't know how to fix it.

FocusRunner built a 4-step AI qualification funnel: (1) Landing page with before/after gallery and price range transparency, (2) AI chatbot that asks 5 qualifying questions (budget, timeline, procedure interest), (3) Automated SMS follow-up with Dr. Chen's availability, (4) Pre-consultation education sequence to warm leads before they walk in. Same ad spend. Completely different result.
