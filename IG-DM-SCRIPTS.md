# Med Spa Instagram DM Outreach Scripts — AI Patient Acquisition

**Owner:** Copywriter (Schwartz Framework)
**Target:** Med spa owners, spa managers, aesthetics practitioners
**Channel:** Instagram DM (primary cold outreach channel for med spa owners)
**Tone:** Peer-to-peer. Agency owner to spa owner. No marketer smell.
**Rule:** First DM under 150 characters. No links in first message.
**Language:** English only. Board mandate.

---

## The Schwartz Framework Applied

Every DM below follows Eugene Schwartz's 4 Forces:

1. **Desire** — They want more booked patients without hiring more staff
2. **Identification** — They see themselves in the situation described
3. **Credibility** — Numbers, case studies, specific observations
4. **Action** — Clear next step that costs nothing

---

## Hook 1: The Cost-Per-Booked (Highest conversion)

**Step 1 — Icebreaker (under 150 chars):**

> [Name] — quick math question. Roughly what's your cost per booked consult right now?

**Step 2 — Follow-up (only if they reply):**

> Most med spa owners I talk to are at $300-400/booking. That's not an ad problem — it's a response speed problem.
>
> We've got a system that drops that to under $150. Same ads. Same traffic. Same staff. Just faster follow-up.
>
> One of our Scottsdale clients went from $381 to $135 per booked. Added $34K in month one.
>
> Open to a 10-minute look at how?

**Schwartz rationale:** The specific number hooks attention. The comparison ($381→$135) creates desire. The result ($34K) provides proof. The ask (10 minutes) reduces friction.

**Schwartz rationale:** FOMO is the strongest desire trigger in med spa. Market share capture creates urgency. The 78% stat is counterintuitive — drives identification.

## Hook 3: The Front Desk Leak (Operational pain)

**Step 1 — Icebreaker:**

> [Name] — how many hours/week does your front desk spend answering "how much is Botox?" in DMs?

**Step 2 — Follow-up:**

> I've asked 47 med spa owners this quarter. Average answer: 12+ hours/week. That's $1,200+/month in payroll answering questions that 80% of the time don't book.
>
> Your front desk isn't lazy — they're trapped in a broken system.
>
> We put AI in front of every inbound lead. It qualifies spend, timing, treatment type. Only ready-to-book patients reach a human.
>
> Our clients see 30-50% more booked consults. Zero extra staff.
>
> $2.5K/mo — less than a part-time receptionist. Worth a look?

**Schwartz rationale:** The $1,200/month waste is a specific pain point every med spa owner feels. Schwartz says "specify the pain to make it real." The comparison to a part-time receptionist makes the price feel cheap.

## Script Selection Guide

| Owner Profile | Best Hook | Why |
|--------------|-----------|-----|
| Running ads $3K+/mo, low booking rate | Hook 1 — Cost Per Booked | Directly ties ad spend to lost revenue |
| Busy, staff maxed out | Hook 3 — Front Desk Leak | Saves payroll + headache |
| Confident, "we're fine" | Hook 4 — Challenger Insight | Reframes their problem |
| Competitive market | Hook 2 — 2 AM Lead | FOMO + first-mover math |
| High-value target ($2M+ rev) | Hook 5 — Specific Observation | Shows you did homework |
| Seen DM, no reply in 48h | Voice Message | Direct close with guarantee |

---

## Conversion Rules

1. **One hook per contact.** Don't send multiple DMs in different threads.
2. **7-day gap between sequences.** If Hook 1 gets no reply, wait 7 days, try Hook 3.
3. **Voice message = one shot.** Use only after 48h of silence on a seen DM.
4. **If they reply with a number or question → reply immediately.** Speed matters in the DM too.
5. **No links in first message.** Instagram flags accounts that DM-link strangers. Step 2 links only.
6. **Track conversion.** Log each DM outcome: replied / interested / booked-demo / not-interested / no-reply.
