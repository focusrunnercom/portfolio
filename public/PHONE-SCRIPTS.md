# SCRIPT 1: Cold Call — 60 Seconds

**Goal:** Book a 15-minute discovery call
**Target:** Med spa owner answering their own phone

**YOU:** "Hi [Name]? This is [Name] calling from FocusRunner. I'm reaching out to med spa owners in Miami because we built something that doubled consult bookings for a clinic in Brickell without spending a dollar more on ads."

**THEM:** (hesitation / "what is it?")

**YOU:** "Quick question — what's your lead response time right now? Most med spas I talk to say 12 to 24 hours."

**THEM:** (answers — usually "yeah, about that")

**YOU:** "That's exactly what we fix. We installed an AI lead qualifier on a local med spa's site. Same ads, same leads — but response time went from 18 hours to under 5 minutes. Their consult bookings tripled from 12 to 31 a month."

**YOU:** "I'd love to show you how it works. Do you have 15 minutes this week for a quick call? No pitch — just show you what it looks like on your site."

**HOLD FOR ANSWER.** If yes → book on calendar. If no → Script 3 (Objection).

# SCRIPT 2: Follow-Up Call — Lead Magnet Delivery

**Goal:** Deliver value, close for paid call
**Trigger:** They downloaded the Free Patient Acquisition Audit or showed interest

**YOU:** "Hi [Name], [Name] from FocusRunner. You downloaded our Patient Acquisition Audit last week — wanted to share something I think you'll find valuable."

**YOU:** "We just published a data analysis of 47 med spas. The finding: response speed correlates with conversion more than ad spend, price, or location. Med spas responding within 5 minutes convert at 31%. Those responding within 24 hours? Under 5%."

**YOU:** "Here's the part I wanted to flag for you specifically —"

**TRANSITION OPTIONS:**
- If they have a lead capture form: "→ your form goes to email → you see it next morning. That 12-hour gap is costing you roughly 20% conversion right off the top."
- If they use Instagram DMs: "→ DMs don't notify instantly unless you're in the app. That 2-hour average response time is leaking 15% of your leads."
- If they take phone calls: "→ phone is great but you can't be on it 24/7. We put an AI in front that handles the first 3 questions."

**YOU:** "I'd like to set up a 15-minute screen share this week. I'll show you the exact lead qualifier running on a Miami med spa site right now — takes 30 minutes to install on yours."

**HOLD FOR ANSWER.** Book or handle objection.

# SCRIPT 3: Objection Handling — 5 Objections + Rebuttals

## Objection 1: "We already get enough leads / our conversion is fine"
**REBUTTAL:** "That's good. Most med spa owners say the same thing — until they measure it. We ran an audit for a Brickell clinic that thought they were converting 25%. Actual number was 12% once we tracked from first click to booked consult. Would you be open to a 5-minute audit of your actual numbers? Free."

## Objection 2: "I don't want AI talking to my patients"
**REBUTTAL:** "That's fair. The AI doesn't replace your front desk — it handles exactly 3 qualification questions: service interest, budget range, and timing. Only hot leads reach a human. Everyone else gets a nurture email. Most patients actually prefer it — they get answers at 2 AM instead of waiting until morning."

## Objection 3: "How much does it cost?"
**REBUTTAL:** "$250/month. That's less than the cost of a single Google Ads click in Miami for med spa keywords. Most clients see ROI in the first week from recovering one or two leads they would have lost."

## Objection 4: "I don't have time to set this up"
**REBUTTAL:** "That's the point — I set it up for you. 30 minutes, one line of code on your existing page, done. After that it runs itself. You don't touch it. Leads come in, AI qualifies them, your phone buzzes for hot ones only."

## Objection 5: "Send me info and I'll think about it"
**REBUTTAL:** "I absolutely can. But here's the thing — every day you wait, you're losing 70% of the leads you're already paying for. I've seen the data across 47 med spas. The ones who fix response speed win. The ones who wait keep spending the same ad budget for half the results. I'll send you the blog post with the data — but let me also book 15 minutes to show you the actual system running. Tuesday or Thursday?"
