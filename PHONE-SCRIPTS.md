# PHONE SCRIPTS — Miami Med Spa Cold Calling
## For Sales Agent (FOC-454)
## Framework: SPIN Selling + Challenger Model
## Target: Med spa owners $500K-$2M, Miami-Dade
## CTA: focusrunner.io/lead-capture (Free Audit)

---

## SCRIPT 1: COLD CALL — 60 Seconds

**Goal:** Establish credibility → Name specific pain → Move to 15-min discovery call.

**OPENING (10 seconds)**
"Hi [Name], this is [Sales] at FocusRunner. I'm going to be direct: you're spending $3K-$5K a month on ads, and 70% of those leads never book. We fix that in 48 hours."

**TRANSITION (10 seconds)**
"Quick question — are you seeing leads come in but disappear before they schedule?"

**LISTEN** — let them answer. Common responses:

- "We're too busy to follow up" → "That's exactly what we solve. Instant response so no lead falls through."
- "We just don't have the front desk bandwidth" → "Good — that means you have plenty of leads. The fix takes 48 hours."
- "We don't have that problem" → "Would you be open to a 2-minute free audit that shows your actual number? Most owners are surprised."

**CLOSE (15 seconds)**
"Here's what I propose: a 15-minute call where I walk you through your current numbers vs. what you'd recover with instant response. No pitch, no contract — just the data. Wednesday or Thursday at 10 AM?"

**IF YES:** "Perfect — I'll send a calendar link. Look for the Free Lead Audit email from me."

**IF NO/NOT NOW:** "Understood. I'll send you our Free Audit link — it shows where your pipeline is leaking in under 2 minutes. If the numbers surprise you, my calendar's open."

## SCRIPT 2: FOLLOW-UP CALL — Lead Magnet Delivery

**Goal:** Warm follow-up on the Free Audit → Case study → Close for paid discovery.

**OPENING (15 seconds)**
"Hi [Name], [Sales] from FocusRunner. You ran our Free Lead Audit last week — curious what it showed you. Did the numbers line up with what you expected?"

**LISTEN.** Three paths:

**PATH A — They engaged with the audit:**
"Most owners tell us the leak is worse than they thought. Here's a real case: A Miami clinic your size was losing 80% of web leads. Add spend was $5K/month, bookings were 12/mo. After our AI qualification pipeline, they went to 47 bookings per month — same spend. Same staff. Different response speed.

Can I show you what that same setup looks like for your practice? 15-minute call, no pressure."

**PATH B — They haven't opened the audit:**
"No problem — I can walk you through it live. It takes 3 minutes. I'll show you exactly where your leads are leaking and what recovery would mean for your monthly revenue."

**PATH C — They're not interested:**
"No worries. I'll leave the audit report with your team. If anything changes — ad spend goes up, you're frustrated with lead quality — my number is in the audit email."

## SCRIPT 3: OBJECTION HANDLING — 5 Rebuttals

**Goal:** Handle the top 5 objections smoothly and steer back to discovery.

### OBJECTION 1: "We already have a system."
**Rebut:** "Most systems handle follow-up during business hours. Ours works 24/7 — leads at 2 AM get the same response as leads at 2 PM. Quick test: submit a form to your site right now. How fast does someone respond? If it's more than 5 minutes, you're losing money."

### OBJECTION 2: "We can't afford it right now."
**Rebut:** "I understand. But you're spending $3K-$5K on ads and getting 30% of the value. Our clients recover their monthly investment within 5-8 booked appointments. If you're generating 50 leads/month, even a 30% recovery pays for itself. Let me show you the math — 15 minutes, no obligation."

### OBJECTION 3: "Our patients find us through referrals."
**Rebut:** "Referrals are great — highest LTV patient you can get. But that's the patients you already had. What about the patients who don't know you exist? Every dollar you spend on ads sends leads to your competitors if you don't answer fast enough. The system doesn't replace referrals. It captures the traffic you're already paying for."

### OBJECTION 4: "We're too busy to take a call."
**Rebut:** "I get it — that means business is good. But it also means there are leads falling through the cracks that your front desk doesn't have time to chase. Would 15 minutes this week be worth seeing how much revenue you're leaving on the table? Most clinics find $10K-$15K/month in recoverable leads."

### OBJECTION 5: "Send me info and I'll look at it."
**Rebut (don't ghost):** "Happy to. I'll send you: (1) the Free Audit link, (2) a one-page case study from a Miami clinic your size. You'll see the exact before/after numbers. My calendar is open if the data makes you curious. But just so I know — what's the #1 frustration with your current lead flow?"

**Key principle:** Never let "send me info" end the conversation. Get one specific pain point on record so you can follow up with targeted value.
