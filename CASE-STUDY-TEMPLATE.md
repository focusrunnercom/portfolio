# Med Spa AI Case Study Template

**Format:** 1-page PDF + blog post version
**Purpose:** Used in retargeting ads, email sequences, and as social proof on the landing page
**Tone:** First-person (practice owner voice). Numbers-driven. Before/after structure.

---

## Template Structure

### 1. The Headline

Format: `How [Practice Name] Went From [Before Metric] to [After Metric] in [Timeframe] — Without Changing Their Ads`

> Example: How Scottsdale Med Spa Went From 14 to 39 Booked Patients Per Month — Without Changing a Single Ad

### 2. The Situation (2-3 sentences)

> [Practice Name] is a [1,2,3]-location med spa in [City] offering [Botox, filler, laser, RF microneedling, etc.]. They were spending $[X]/mo on [Meta/Google] ads and getting [Y] leads — but only booking [Z] appointments per month.

Key data points to collect:
- Ad spend per month
- Lead volume per month
- Booking rate (%)
- Number of locations
- Primary treatments offered

### 3. The Problem (2-3 sentences)

Focus on the root cause — always a pipeline problem, not an ad problem:

> Their booking rate was stuck at [X]%. We traced it to [slow response time / no follow-up / unqualified intake]. Their front desk was taking [X] hours to respond to new leads, and [X]% of leads received zero follow-up after the first contact.

### 4. The Fix (3-4 sentences, bullet format)

> We deployed the AI Patient Acquisition System across [channels]:
> - AI chat widget on website (2:1 response time down to <10 seconds)
> - SMS intake with qualification routing (high-value leads → immediate call)
> - 5-touch follow-up sequence (email + SMS over 15 days)
> - Meta DM auto-response with booking link

Be specific about what was configured. Avoid jargon.

### 5. The Results (the core — use bold numbers)

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Monthly ad spend | $[X] | $[X] | Same |
| Monthly leads | [X] | [X] | [X]% |
| Booking rate | [X]% | [X]% | +[X] pts |
| Cost per booked appointment | $[X] | $[X] | -[X]% |
| Monthly revenue from new patients | $[X] | $[X] | +$[X] |

**Bold takeaway:**
> Before AI: $4,500 ad spend → 14 bookings → $381 per booked
> After AI: $4,500 ad spend → 39 bookings → $115 per booked
> Net gain: +$24,500/month in revenue from the same ad spend

### 6. The Quote (1 quote from the practice owner/manager)

> [Practice Name], they said:
>
> "[Full quote — 2-3 sentences. Should focus on the business impact, not the technology. Something like: 'I thought my problem was not enough leads. Turns out I had plenty — I was just losing them to slow response. The system paid for itself in the first week.']"
> — [Name], [Title], [Practice Name]

### 7. The CTA

> If you're spending $[X]K+ on ads and booking <20% of leads, the same system can work for your practice. Let us run your numbers.
>
> [Book Your 15-Minute Walkthrough →]

## Style Guide for Case Studies

- **Never use stock photos** — use real practice photos (exterior, treatment room, or owner headshot with permission)
- **Numbers in every paragraph** — no claim without a number to back it up
- **No superlatives** — no "amazing," "incredible," "game-changing." Let the numbers speak
- **Before/after must be same ad spend** — the whole point is we don't touch ads
- **Keep it to 1 page** — PDF format. Blog version can be expanded
- **Publish on focusrunner.io** in /case-studies/ section (create page per case study)
