**Owner:** Copywriter
**Framework:** Schwartz — Force 1 (Desire) + Force 4 (Action) dominant
**Channel:** Google Search Ads + Meta Ads (Feed + Stories)
**Tone:** Urgent, peer-to-peer. These ads target med spa owners in the exact moment they're checking their booking numbers.
**Language:** English only. Board mandate.
**Date:** 2026-05-13

---

## Strategy Note (Schwartz)

Most med spa ads target **need** ("Get more patients"). We target **fear + envy** — the specific terror of knowing a patient messaged you at 2 AM and your competitor answered.

The trigger moment: Sunday night. A med spa owner checks their phone. They see a notification: "Someone asked about Botox." Sent 7 hours ago. No reply.

That's where these ads land.

## Deployment Notes

- **Ad 1 & 4:** Always-on search ads. $50-100/day budget each.
- **Ad 3 & 5:** Retargeting + prospecting. Instagram Stories + Reels. $30-50/day.
- **Ad 6:** Search ad for high-intent queries ("med spa cost per booking," "reduce cost per consult"). Low budget ($20/day), high intent.
- **Ad 7:** Weekly cadence. Launch Sunday night (when med spa owners are planning their week). Pause after slots fill.
